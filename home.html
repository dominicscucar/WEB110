I have been working in the Information Technology field since graduating from Johnson County Community College. After attending Ottawa University-Kansas City, I have worked primarily with the Microsoft technology stack. Currently I am taking classes at Johnson County Community College to make a career change from a Back-end Software Developer to a Full Stack Developer.

Although I have done some website development on my own, for fun, and have always enjoyed it, I have never had traditional training in it, so I decided to get back to the basics. This website is a work in progress, so right now it will render well only on desktop monitors, but as my skills grow, it will render well on mobile devices as well.

I have done some searching over the past three years, seeing if there was another field of work that I should get into, but I realized that software development is the only career that I have ever really been passionate about. My career was exceptionally memorable between 2008 and 2018, when I was working in the mortgage lending industry, and I would like to make some of those good memories again in the near future.

I have always lived in the Kansas City metropolitan area and have created my website with a Kansas City theme. In my spare time I enjoy spending time with family, listening to classic rock, jazz and classical music, bowling, researching genealogy and reading.
